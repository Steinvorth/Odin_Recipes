Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of wide flat pasta noodles, alternating with a rich tomato-based sauce, seasoned ground meat such as beef, pork, or sausage, creamy ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. Additional ingredients such as vegetables like spinach, zucchini, or eggplant, and herbs like basil or oregano can also be added to the layers. The layers are typically baked in the oven until the top is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly. The result is a hearty, comforting and flavorful meal that is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package (9-12 ounces) of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 jar (24-26 ounces) of pasta sauce
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 container (15 ounces) of ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. n a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage until it is browned and no longer pink. Drain off any excess fat.
  4. Add the jar of pasta sauce, diced tomatoes, minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine and let the mixture simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, and the egg. Stir well to combine.
  6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of the dish.
  7. Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, overlapping slightly to cover the entire surface.
  8. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  9. Add a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the ricotta cheese.
  10. Repeat steps 7-9, alternating layers of meat sauce, noodles, ricotta cheese mixture, and mozzarella cheese until you have used up all of the ingredients.
  11. Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese on top of the final layer of mozzarella cheese.
  12. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is golden brown and bubbly.
  14. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
